En Passant

En passant is a special pawn capture that can occur when a pawn
moves two squares forward on its first move and lands next to an
opponent's pawn on its fifth rank. The opponent's pawn can then
capture the first pawn as if it had only moved one square forward.
This move can only be made immediately after the first pawn has
moved two squares, and only if it is the first pawn's very next move.

Castling

Castling is a special move that allows the king and one
of the rooks to move at the same time, towards each
other, and switch places. Castling can only be done once
per game and only under specific conditions: the king
and rook must not have moved yet, there can be no pieces
between them, the king cannot be in check, and the squares
the king passes over and lands on must not be under attack
by any of the opponent's pieces. Castling is a valuable move
that helps the king to move to safety and the rook to become more active.
